# Why Full On-Chain Trading

## What it is

Full on-chain trading is a system design where the core trading logic, balance updates, and settlement process all run on-chain.

It is different from both centralized exchanges and AMM-based DEXs.

## Why it matters

Crypto trading has long been divided between two models:

- Centralized exchanges offer smooth user experience and fast execution, but require custody and trust in opaque systems.
- AMM-based DEXs offer transparency and self-custody, but often sacrifice execution precision and trading flexibility.

A full on-chain trading system aims to combine usability with verifiability.

## How it works

SSS explores this model through:

- Internal ledger design
- Orderbook-style trading
- Limit orders with partial fills
- Verifiable on-chain settlement

This architecture is designed to create a smoother trading experience while keeping the system auditable on-chain.

## Summary

- Full on-chain trading is not the same as AMM trading
- It aims to combine UX and trust
- SSS is building toward this architecture on ICP
