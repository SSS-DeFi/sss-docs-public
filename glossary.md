# Glossary

## Internal Ledger

A balance model maintained within the system for smoother interaction and trading flow.

## Orderbook-style Trading

A trading model focused on explicit pricing and execution logic rather than pure AMM curve behavior.

## Fully On-Chain Trading System

A system where the core trading logic and settlement behavior are executed on-chain.

## Public Test

A stage where the product is available for use and feedback, but still evolving.

## Verifiability

The ability to inspect or confirm system behavior through on-chain records and transparent logic.
