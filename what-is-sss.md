# What is SSS

## What it is

SSS is a fully on-chain trading system built on the Internet Computer (ICP).

## Why it matters

Crypto trading has long been split between centralized exchanges and AMM-based DEXs. SSS explores a third path: combining smooth trading experience with verifiable on-chain execution.

## How it works

SSS introduces:
- Internal ledger
- Orderbook-style execution
- Limit orders with partial fills
- On-chain settlement

## Summary

- Not a traditional CEX
- Not a typical AMM DEX
- A fully on-chain trading system
