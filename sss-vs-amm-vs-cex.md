# SSS vs AMM vs CEX

## What it is

This page explains how SSS differs from traditional centralized exchanges and AMM-based decentralized exchanges.

## Why it matters

Many users understand crypto trading through the lens of only two options:

- CEX
- DEX

But SSS is better understood as a different category: a fully on-chain trading system.

## How it works

### CEX

Centralized exchanges typically provide:

- Smooth user experience
- Fast execution
- Deep liquidity

But users must trust the operator and give up direct control over custody.

### AMM-based DEX

AMM DEXs typically provide:

- On-chain transparency
- Self-custody
- Open participation

But they are often shaped by liquidity curves and can be less flexible for advanced trading.

### SSS

SSS is designed to provide:

- CEX-like trading experience
- On-chain verifiability
- Internal ledger architecture
- Orderbook-style execution
- Limit orders with partial fills

SSS is not a traditional CEX.
SSS is not a typical AMM DEX.
SSS is a fully on-chain trading system.

## Summary

- CEX focuses on convenience
- AMM DEX focuses on openness
- SSS aims to combine user experience with on-chain trust
