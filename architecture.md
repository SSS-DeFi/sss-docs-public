# Architecture

## What it is

This page introduces the high-level architecture of SSS.

## Why it matters

SSS is not a typical AMM DEX. Its design is better understood through architecture rather than through token pairs alone.

## How it works

SSS is designed around several core ideas:

- Internal ledger architecture
- Orderbook-style trading
- On-chain settlement
- Verifiable execution
- Separation between fast feedback and finalized state

This allows the system to move toward a smoother trading experience while remaining auditable on-chain.

## Summary

- SSS is architecture-led
- It is not based purely on AMM curves
- Its design aims to combine UX and trust
