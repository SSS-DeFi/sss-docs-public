# FAQ

## What makes SSS different?

SSS focuses on fully on-chain trading with internal ledger design and orderbook-style execution.

## Is SSS a DEX or a CEX?

SSS is not a traditional CEX and not a typical AMM DEX. It is better understood as a fully on-chain trading system.

## Why ICP?

ICP provides the performance and on-chain execution environment needed for this system design.

## Is SSS already live?

SSS is currently in public test.
